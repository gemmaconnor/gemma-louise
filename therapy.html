Therapy

Following initial assessment I will provide an honest opinion as to whether your child will benefit from therapy sessions and an action plan will be agreed.

Therapy Sessions

Therapy sessions are play based and can be offered on weekly or fortnightly basis depending on your child’s needs. Therapy sessions last 45 minutes or 1 hour in duration.

Towards the end of each session time is spent with parents discussing ideas and activities for home practice between sessions. I ensure that home activities can be easily managed within your daily routine.

Sessions can take place at your home. All therapy programmes are tailored to each individual child’s needs. I am also able to provide video therapy sessions via Zoom and other platforms.

We can help children facing challenges which include:


Speech sound difficulties (pronunciation)
Talking and using Language
Understanding spoken language

Communication difficulties associated with syndromes or learning difficulties
Stammering (fluency)
Delayed attention , listening and play skills
